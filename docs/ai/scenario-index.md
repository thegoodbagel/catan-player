# Scenario Index (working catalogue)

Status: partial. Official games described from general knowledge of published
rules; fan maps catalogued from catancollector.com page text only (layouts live
in images we could not read). A background research pass is expanding this under
`docs/ai/maps/`. Verify against rulebooks before encoding into data.

## Official base game + expansions

- **Base game** — 5 resources, 19 hexes, robber, 10 VP. The reference scenario.
- **Seafarers** — adds sea hexes, ships (road over water), gold fields, the pirate
  (sea robber), and scenario VPs (e.g. settling new islands). Multiple scenarios
  with different island layouts.
- **Cities & Knights** — adds commodities (coin/cloth/paper), city improvements,
  knights, city walls, the barbarian threat (event die + barbarian track), and
  progress cards. Typically a higher VP target (commonly 13).
- **Traders & Barbarians** — a bundle of smaller scenarios (e.g. caravans, fishing,
  rivers/bridges, barbarian attack) plus rules for friendlier/teaching play.
- **Explorers & Pirates** — exploration of fog/unknown tiles, ships that carry
  crews/settlers, missions/objectives, pirate combat.
